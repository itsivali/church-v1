Harvest Land Ministries International (HLMI)

We are a non-profit, non-denominational, non-political, faith based organization founded by Dr William O.Ouma in 1989 with the aim of Reconciling and Transforming communities holistically by Evangelizing the lost with the gospel of Jesus Christ for purposes of salvation and to effectively disciple believers and plant Churches that assist in the spiritual growth and development of the believers. Also, to raise, develop, train and equip Christian and community leaders for effective transformational leadership, and to fight against discrimination and help provide support and care for the marginalized, less fortunate, and the vulnerable such as street children, orphans, widows, Persons Living With Disability, women, youth, and the Elderly. HLMI’s aim also, is to address the issues of peace, inadequate human care services, poverty, and underdevelopment in such areas as socio-economic/micro-financing, livelihood, agriculture and food security, Humanitarian and Disaster management, medical, health, nutritional and psychosocial services, water and sanitation, Education, and Justice and advocacy especially in the area of violence against women and girls.
